Uniqtrade

We cooperate with many, reputable, distinguished steel mills and supply our clients with steel from Taiwan, China, Korea, India, Russia, etc. Due to direct cooperation with mills we are flexible regarding prices as well as the terms and conditions of the supply. Offered material is of very good quality and accordant with international quality standards (EN, JIS, ASTM, etc) Our mission is the highest customer satisfaction with delivered service and the quality of steel material.

Advantages:

  • Shipment to ports in Poland: Gdynia, Gdańsk, and to the most of the seaports in the world (by containers, bulk)
  • We assist with arranging of import licenses to Poland, clearance of material at the border and other documents.
  • We offer the possibility of renting a warehouse space in Polish ports.
  • We secure the possibility of visiting the mill in order to show the production of ordered steel.
  • Quality control of the purchased material throughout the whole process of production.
  • We offer prime quality material, ordered directly at the mill according to the specification of the purchaser.
  • Competitive prices.
  • Well known, and commonly used material.
  • Wide range of the possibilities of financing.
  • Material is insured by the international insurers companies.
  • Professional, complex service from Poland.

Our offer:

Pre-painted steel in coils:

  • Steel grade: DX51, S250, S280, S320, and others.
  • Protective layer: Zn(PPGI), alucynk (55% Al.-Zn - PPGL), galfan (5%Al-Zn)
  • Coils: 900mm – 1500mm
  • Thickness (Feedstock material): 0.20mm – 1.20mm
  • Protective lacquer layer (optional ):
    • PE - standard polyester – two layers: prime: 5um (Epoxy) + topcoat: 20um;
    • PRINTED - steel in different patterns: for example: wooden, brick, stone pattern, etc.
    • SMP: - polyester mixed with silicone - primer: 5um + SMP
    • PVDF - Polyvinylidene Fluoride: primer: 5um + PVDF
    • PET / PCM laminate: 20 - 50 um.
    • BAROQUE EMBOSSED: - coated with material with similar characteristic to PVC (Polyvinyl chloride)

Hot dipped galvanized steel (coils, sheets, strips):

  • Steel grade: Q195, DX51, S250, S280, S320,etc.
  • Zinc layer: Z80 – Z350 g/m2
  • Width: 15mm - 1500mm

  • Steel grade: Q195, DX51, S250, S280, S320,etc.
  • Aluzinc layer: AZ150 – AZ180 g/m2
  • Width: 900mm - 1500mm
  • Protective anti-finger film (AFP)

Cold rolled steel (coils, sheets, strips):

  • Steel grade: DC01 and others

Hot rolled steel (coils, sheets, strips):

  • Steel grade: S235JR and others

Steel tubes and profiles (Hot rolled and hot dipped galvanized)
